About Us

Dhakadam Mahila Sanakisan Sahakari Sanstha Ltd. is a women's small farmers' cooperative located in Darma-6, Triveni, Salyan, Nepal. This cooperative plays a significant role in empowering local women, particularly in agriculture and farming activities, by providing them with opportunities for economic and social upliftment.

The cooperative is driven by the mission to improve the livelihoods of its members through collaborative farming, financial support, and capacity-building programs. It offers savings and credit services, enabling women farmers to access financial resources for investing in agricultural activities, improving their yields, and generating income. The cooperative also focuses on enhancing skills through training in modern farming techniques, financial literacy, and entrepreneurship, allowing members to contribute more effectively to their households and communities.

As a community-based organization, Dhakadam Mahila Sanakisan Sahakari Sanstha Ltd. promotes the principles of self-help, mutual benefit, and sustainable development. Through this platform, the women of Triveni have not only become economically independent but have also strengthened their social standing and participation in decision-making processes within their community.
